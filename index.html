About

Dr. Ioannis Giannoulis serves as a Lecturer in Accounting and Finance at the Hellenic Mediterranean University (HMU) , Department of Accounting and Finance.

He teaches core undergraduate and postgraduate courses in areas such as Financial Accounting, Taxation, and Auditing, combining long academic experience with practical understanding of the Greek financial environment.

Over the years, Dr. Giannoulis has contributed to the university community through active roles in academic coordination, internship supervision, and participation in institutional committees. His approach focuses on bridging academic knowledge with real-world business and accounting practice.

He holds degrees from Lancaster University (UK), Athens University of Economics and Business, and Frederick University (Cyprus).
