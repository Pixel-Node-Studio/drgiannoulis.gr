About

Dr. Ioannis K. Giannoulis is a Lecturer in Accounting and Finance at the Hellenic Mediterranean University with extensive experience in corporate governance, financial markets, and accounting education. His research focuses on the intersection of business law and finance, particularly in emerging markets.

With degrees from Lancaster University, Athens University of Economics and Business, and Frederick University, Dr. Giannoulis brings an international perspective to both his research and teaching practices.
